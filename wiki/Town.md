# 🏘️ Town Mechanics

Floor 0 is your safe haven, the Town. Here you can prepare for your long delve into the Endless Abyss.

## Town Services
- **The Innkeeper (`H`)**: Will fully heal your HP for a moderate amount of Gold.
- **The Blacksmith (`B`)**: Can sharpen your weapons or reinforce your armor, granting permanent +1 ATK or +1 DEF bonuses (costs scale drastically per upgrade).
- **The Wizard's Tower (`W`)**: Offers premium enchanting services. You can buy the ESP effect (Telepathy) temporarily, or fully Identify all gear in your inventory for a hefty sum of Gold.
- **The Bank (`£`)**: Deposit gold here so you don't lose it if you die in the dungeon! Withdrawing is free.

## Dynamic Events
The Town changes wildly depending on Day/Night conditions and random probability:
- **Villagers**: Wander aimlessly, sharing rumors.
- **Beggars (`b`)**: Can be given 5 Gold. Sometimes they just thank you; occasionally, they will Identify a random item in your backpack for free!
- **The Town Well (`#`)**: A pristine water source. Drinking from it might grant a minor heal, or on rare occasions, slightly boost a core stat permanently.
- **The Gambler's Den (`G`)**: Spawns randomly. Pay 50 Gold to roll the dice. You could win 200 Gold, or you could anger the Gambler's thugs!
- **The Mayor's Bounty (`M`)**: The Mayor will occasionally post a bounty for a specific monster type. Killing that monster in the dungeon grants double Gold and XP for the duration of the run.

## Access
You can return to the Town at any point by reading a **Scroll of Word of Recall** (sold occasionally by the Goblin Merchant or found on early dungeon floors). Returning resets your depth to Floor 1!
