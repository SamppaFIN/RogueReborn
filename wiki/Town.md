# 🏘️ Town Mechanics

Floor 0 is your safe haven, the Town. Here you can prepare for your long delve into the Endless Abyss.

## Phase I Services
- **The Innkeeper (`H`)**: Will fully heal your HP for a moderate amount of Gold.
- **The Blacksmith (`B`)**: Can sharpen your weapons or reinforce your armor, granting permanent +1 ATK or +1 DEF bonuses (costs scale drastically per upgrade).
- **The Wizard's Tower (`W`)**: Offers premium enchanting services. You can buy the ESP effect (Telepathy) temporarily, or fully Identify all gear in your inventory for a hefty sum of Gold.
- **The Bank (`£`)**: Deposit gold here so you don't lose it if you die in the dungeon! Withdrawing is free.

## Phase II Expansion Services
- **The Alchemist (`A`)**: Sells an assortment of potions. Can transmute 2 Minor Healing Potions into 1 Greater Healing Potion for 50g.
- **The Class Trainer (`T`)**: Offers permanent stat increases (+10 Max HP, +1 ATK, +1 DEF) for a large sum of gold.
- **The Cartographer (`C`)**: Sells dungeon intel, providing you the exact floor and species of an Elite/Boss monster for 100g.

## Meta-Progression
- **The Guildhall (`{`)**: A hallowed hall recording the Top 10 run scores across all lifetimes.
- **The Stash (`[`)**: Persistent Storage. You can store up to 5 items safely across any number of deaths.

## Dynamic Events
The Town changes wildly depending on Day/Night conditions and random probability:
- **Villagers**: Wander aimlessly, sharing rumors.
- **Beggars (`p`)**: Can be given 5 Gold. Sometimes they just thank you; occasionally, they will Identify a random item in your backpack for free!
- **The Town Well (`O`)**: A pristine water source. Drinking from it might grant a minor heal, or on rare occasions, slightly boost a core stat permanently.
- **The Gambler's Den (`7`)**: Spawns randomly at Night. Pay 50 Gold to roll the dice. You could win random items or rarer loot.
- **The Mayor's Bounty (`6`)**: The Mayor will occasionally post a bounty for a specific monster type.
- **Dynamic Weather**: The town has a chance to experience Rain or Fog, slightly restricting visibility and speed.
- **Town Invasions**: There is a 10% chance that Goblins raid the town upon your arrival, forcing you into combat in the safe zone!

## Access
You can return to the Town at any point by reading a **Scroll of Word of Recall** (sold occasionally by the Goblin Merchant or found on early dungeon floors). Returning resets your depth to Floor 1!
