# 🐲 Bestiary

Rogue Reborn features over 25 unique monsters with different behaviors, mechanics, and stats.

## The Core Fiends (Floors 1-6)
- **Rat** `r`: Base enemy. Weak, fast.
- **Goblin** `g`: Standard humanoid.
- **Kobold** `k`: Slightly stronger than Goblins.
- **Fire Hound** `h`: Deals fire damage, high speed.
- **Orc** `o`: Beefy melee attacker.
- **Dark Elf** `e`: Fast magic user.
- **Giant Spider** `s`: Fast, poisons on hit.

## The Mid-Depths (Floors 4-8)
- **Gelatinous Cube** `C`: Invisible unless directly adjacent. High HP.
- **Vampire** `V`: 20% chance to drain maximum HP permanently on hit.
- **Necromancer** `N`: Summons Skeletons `Z` during combat.
- **Skeleton** `Z`: High defense, spawned by Necromancers.
- **Rust Monster** `X`: Degrades your equipped Armor (-1 DEF) permanently on hit!
- **Blink Dog** `n`: Teleports randomly when damaged below 50% HP.
- **Cave Troll** `T`: Massive health and physical damage. Slow.

## The Abyss & Beyond (Floors 8+)
- **Wraith** `W`: Drains HP and magic stats.
- **Beholder** `I`: Fires an Eye-Ray that can randomly Slow, Confuse, or Blind the player from a distance.
- **Mind Flayer** `M`: Drains raw Experience Points (XP) drastically slowing your leveling progress.
- **Dragon** `D`: Breathes fire, massive health pool.
- **Balrog** `B`: The ultimate fiery beast (Floor 10 standard spawn).

## 👑 Mini-Bosses & Elites
- **Golden Elites**: A 10% chance for any normal monster to spawn as a glowing yellow Elite. They have 1.5× HP, 1.3× Attack, grant 2× XP, and have a 50% chance to drop a Dungeon Key (`!`).
- **Arch-Lich (`L`)**: Spawns definitively on Floor 8. 80 HP, massive magical attacks. Drains XP and summons lesser undead.
- **Dragon King (`K`)**: Spawns definitively on Floor 9. 120 HP, breathes fire, drains Max HP, and fires Beholder debuff rays. Do not engage blindly!
