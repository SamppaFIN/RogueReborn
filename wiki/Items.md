# 🛡️ Arsenal & Items

Rogue Reborn features an exhaustive list of equipment, magical items, and consumables.

## ⚔️ Weaponry
- **Daggers** (`|`): Fast, low damage.
- **Swords** (`|`): Standard melee.
- **Axes** (`|`): Slower, higher damage.
- **Rapiers** (`|`): Very fast, bonus to dodging.
- **Heavy Maces** (`|`): High damage, negative speed.
- **Bows & Heavy Crossbows** (`}`): Allow ranged attacks (`F` key). Heavy Crossbows require a full turn to reload between shots.
- **Spears & Halberds** (`/`): Grants a 2-tile reach attack (`T` key), attacking over adjacent tiles.
- **Paired Daggers**: Deals two distinct strikes per bump attack.
- **Whips**: Occasionally disarms an enemy, heavily reducing their attack power.

## 🛡️ Armor & Shields
- **Armor** (`[`): Leather, Chainmail, Platemail, Mithril. Increases Defense (DEF). Heavier armors reduce Speed.
- **Shields** (`(`): Bucklers, Kite Shields, Tower Shields. Equipped in the dedicated OFFHAND slot. Boosts DEF at slight Speed costs.
- **Mage Robes** (`[`): Grants `SpellBoost`, increasing wand and scroll damage.

## 💍 Rings & Amulets
- **Rings** (`=`): Ring of Protection (+DEF), Ring of Strength (+ATK), Ring of Speed (+Speed), Ring of Burden (Cursed, -Speed).
- **Amulets** (`"`): Amulet of Health (+Max HP), Amulet of ESP (Grants full telepathy, revealing all monsters on the level).

## 🧪 Potions & Scrolls
- **Healing Potions**: Minor, Curing, Greater, Full (cures statuses).
- **Status Potions**: Poison, Slowness, Confusion, Blindness, Paralysis, Regeneration Boost (fast healing for 30 ticks).
- **Scrolls**: Word of Recall (returns to Town), Identify (reveals item stats), Mapping, Enchant Weapon (+ATK), Enchant Armor (+DEF), Fireball (3-tile AoE), Frost Nova (freezes nearby enemies), Confusion.

## 🪄 Wands 
All Wands (`-`) cast their effect to a targeted tile (select with cursor, click to fire).
- **Magic Missile**: Basic flat damage.
- **Fire**: Heavy flame damage.
- **Frost**: Damage + Slow effect (-3 Speed).
- **Lightning**: Extremely high damage with a 60% chance to chain to an adjacent monster.
- **Slow**: Ranged 4-point speed debuff.
- **Haste (Self)**: Grants +4 Speed for 15 ticks.
- **Teleportation (Self)**: Instantly blinks the player.
- **Drain Life**: Heavy damage, heals the player for 50% of damage dealt.
- **Staff of Wizardry**: 15 charges of pure, devastating Arcane blasts.

## 🌟 Rare Artifacts
Extremely rare named items (1% drop rate from high-level monsters):
- **Sting**: Glows blue, massive damage to Orcs/Goblins.
- **Glamdring**: Superior sword, huge ATK bonus.
- **Anduril**: The legendary blade, unparalleled damage output.
